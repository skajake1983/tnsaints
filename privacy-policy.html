Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

The Tennessee Saints ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website located at tnsaints.com. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit our site, how we use it, and your choices regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

If you submit our contact form, you may voluntarily provide:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Your child's name, grade, and school
  • Any message or details you include

We only use this information to respond to your inquiry. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

Information Collected Automatically

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. Google Analytics collects:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or search terms
  • Browser type, device, and operating system
  • Approximate geographic location (city/region level)
  • Anonymous usage data via cookies

Google Analytics does not collect your name, email, or other personally identifiable information. For more details, see Google's Privacy Policy.

2. Cookies

Our site uses cookies placed by Google Analytics to track anonymous visitor behavior. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time, though some site features may not function as intended.

You may also opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to inquiries submitted through our contact form
  • Understand how visitors interact with our website so we can improve it
  • Communicate program updates to families who have contacted us

4. Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics — website traffic analysis
  • Font Awesome (via cdnjs) — icon delivery
  • Cloudflare — DNS and content delivery
  • GitHub Pages — website hosting

Each of these services may collect limited technical data (such as IP addresses) as part of their normal operation. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

5. Children's Privacy

Our website is intended for parents and guardians of youth athletes. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. Contact form submissions regarding a child are made by a parent or guardian. If you believe a child has submitted personal information without parental consent, please contact us so we can remove it.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information you provide. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Choices

  • You may choose not to submit the contact form
  • You may disable cookies in your browser
  • You may opt out of Google Analytics using the browser add-on linked above
  • You may request deletion of any personal information you've provided by emailing us

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
