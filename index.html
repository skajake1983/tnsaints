The Tennessee Saints are a youth Travel basketball team in Middle TN. Passionate about helping kids build community, improve their basketball skills, compete, and grow in their faith. We are here to build disciplined athletes, confident leaders, and a culture families are proud to be part of.

Mission

Our Mission

The Tennessee Saints exist to develop young athletes through faith, discipline, teamwork, and a love for the game of basketball. Our goal is to help players grow not only as competitors, but as leaders, teammates, and young men of character.

Values

Our Core Values

Team over self

Basketball is a team game. We teach players to communicate, trust teammates, and compete together with discipline and effort.

Character over stats

We believe strong character starts with strong values. Our program emphasizes humility, gratitude, and respect both on and off the court.

Christ over everything

Every player should improve through faith. We develop the whole athlete — grounded in purpose, guided by Christ, and growing year after year.
