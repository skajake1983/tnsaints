Our Story

The Tennessee Saints were created to provide young athletes with high-quality basketball development, competitive experiences, and a strong team culture rooted in faith, discipline, and service.

We believe the game is a training ground for life: learning how to work hard, lead well, stay coachable, and compete with integrity.

Core Values

  • Faith first
  • Team over self
  • Effort every day
  • Discipline in the details
  • Respect for coaches, teammates, and opponents
